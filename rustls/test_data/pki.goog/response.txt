    <div class="paragraph">
      <h1 class="paragraph-title glue-headline glue-headline--headline-5">
        Overview &amp; benefits
      </h1>
      <p class="paragraph-content glue-mod-spacer-3-bottom">
        <p>Encryption is an important building block for a safer internet. Google Trust Services provides Transport Layer Security (TLS) certificates for Google services and users helping to authenticate and encrypt internet traffic. The service is built on Google&rsquo;s geographically distributed infrastructure and backed by security and compliance audits helping to provide a transparent, trusted, and reliable Certificate Authority.</p>
      </p>
    </div>
